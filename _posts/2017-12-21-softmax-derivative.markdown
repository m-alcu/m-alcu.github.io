The Kronecker delta (named after Leopold Kronecker) is a function of two variables, usually just positive integers. The function is 1 if the variables are equal, and 0 otherwise:

$$  \delta_{ij} =
\begin{cases}
0  & \text{if $i \neq j$,} \\
1 & \text{if $i = j.$}
\end{cases}$$
